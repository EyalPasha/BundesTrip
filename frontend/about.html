Our Story

BundesTrip was created by football enthusiasts for football enthusiasts who want to experience the unique atmosphere of German football.

Founded in 2023, BundesTrip emerged from a simple problem: planning multi-game football trips across Germany is incredibly complex. Train schedules, match timings, hotel bookings - coordinating everything manually takes hours of planning.

Our founder, a dedicated football tourist, spent countless weekends traveling across Germany to watch matches in different cities. After years of planning these trips manually, the idea for an automated trip planning tool was born.

Our Mission

We believe football is best experienced live, in the stadium, surrounded by passionate fans. Our mission is to make German football more accessible to visitors by removing the complexity of trip planning.

Maximize Football

We optimize your itinerary to help you watch as many matches as possible during your stay in Germany.

Minimize Travel Time

Our algorithms find the most efficient routes between stadiums to reduce travel time and maximize your enjoyment.

Comfortable Stays

We suggest logical hotel changes that minimize packing and unpacking while keeping you close to the action.

Local Experience

Beyond matches, we help you discover the cities and culture that make German football so special.

How It Works

BundesTrip combines real-time football schedules, train timetables, hotel locations, and fan knowledge to create optimized trip plans:

  1. Enter your starting location, date, and duration
  2. Select your favorite leagues and must-see teams
  3. Our algorithm calculates the best possible trip combinations
  4. Choose from multiple trip options with different priorities
  5. Enjoy your perfectly planned football adventure!
